It works similar to a normal bugtracker. However, instead of keeping track of time spent on solving anissue, it focuses on time wasted on living with it.

Have to wait a minute for the cache to clear on every restart? Post it here!

Spent an hour working around a bug everybody else knew about? Post it here!

Spent half a day hitting your head against the keyboard, then reverted the change? Definitely post it here!

Browse issues, propose solutions, and maybe one day the cost a targeted refactoring will be outweighted by the cost of lost time. Even in the eyes of management.
